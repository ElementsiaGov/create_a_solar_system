Reasons for the Restriction

Dear Aros Elyuim members,

We regret to inform you that access to our website has been restricted for all Aros Elyuim members. This decision was made after careful consideration, and we aim to provide transparency regarding the reasons behind it.

Reasons:

  • Security Concerns: We have observed potential security risks associated with Aros Elyuim member accounts accessing our website.
  • Past Incidents: Previous interactions with Aros Elyuim members have occasionally led to disruptions or misunderstandings.
  • Community Protection: As the owner of the Aros Elyuim website, Elementsia prioritizes the well-being and harmony of our platform and user community.
  • Policy Compliance: Some Aros Elyuim members have inadvertently or repeatedly breached our website policies, necessitating this measure.
  • Guidelines Adherence: Certain Aros Elyuim members have not consistently followed our community guidelines, leading to concerns about their integration into our platform.

We understand this decision may be disappointing for some members, but it is crucial to maintain a secure and welcoming environment for all users. We would like to encourage you to reconsider your membership with Aros Elyuim.

Additionally, we recommend exploring other avenues for community engagement and support. There are numerous platforms and communities available, each with its unique atmosphere and opportunities for participation.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to us through our Discord server.

Thank you for your understanding.
