Reasons for the Ban

Dear Aros Elyuim members,

We regret to inform you that access to our website has been restricted for all Aros Elyuim members. This decision was not made lightly, and we would like to provide transparency regarding the reasons behind it.

Reasons:

  • Security Concerns: We have identified potential security risks associated with Aros Elyuim member accounts accessing our website.
  • Previous Incidents: Past experiences with Aros Elyuim members have resulted in disruptive behavior or violations of our terms of service.
  • Protecting Our Community: To ensure the safety and integrity of our platform and user community, we have taken preventive measures.

We understand this decision may be disappointing for some members, but it is necessary to maintain a secure and welcoming environment for all users. We strongly encourage you to reconsider your membership with Aros Elyuim.

Additionally, we recommend considering membership with Axiom, the region that owns and maintains all affiliated websites and platforms. Axiom provides a safe and inclusive environment for all members.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us on our Discord server.

Thank you.
